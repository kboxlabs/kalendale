Welcome, Wanderer.

At the edge of the civilized world lies Kalendale—a town ringed by iron, superstition, and the endless dark of Morrowynn Forest. Beneath its cemetery, catacombs open like a broken jaw into the Crypt of Shattered Oaths.

This codex is a traveler’s primer: a brief account of places worth visiting (or avoiding), the creatures that stalk the borders of the firelight, and the peoples who brave the night.
